Hearthside Ventures

Hearthside Ventures is the umbrella for a decade of creative, community-driven projects built by two engineer-dads with a love for games and great design. From competitive esports to storefront game spaces, we create things that bring people together—with clarity, intention, and care.

Projects

Hearthside Events

Ran competitive Hearthstone leagues and tournaments from 2014 to 2016, offering cash prizes and ladder points that helped Utah players rise through the competitive scene. It was our first venture—fueled by passion, friendships, and a shared love of the game.

Hearthside Games

Our upcoming brick-and-mortar game store and lounge—designed to be a welcoming third space for trading card games, role-playing games, and community play. Launching in 2025, it's where casual meets competitive, and where everyone belongs.
